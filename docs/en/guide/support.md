# Seeking Support

During the process of reading the documentation, if you have any questions about product usage or valuable suggestions to share, you can seek help through the following methods. Our team and community are committed to serving you.

## Community Support
> Please do not share information about your MCPCAN account and other sensitive keys in the community. Our support staff will also not ask for your account information.

● Submit an Issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/Kymo-MCP/mcpcan)<br>
● Join our [Discord](https://discord.gg/AHW6Bjfm4s) community<br>
● Follow our X account and leave us a message<br>
## Contact Us
For matters other than seeking product support.<br>
● Send an email to opensource@kymo.cn
